How it's done

  • Discover
  • Design
  • Build

  • Discover the truth

    Understanding the needs of a project requires careful research, discovery and analysis.

    Researching your organization's history, competition and sector help us to understand your business the way you do. Through discovery, we seek to directly engage stakeholders and prospective users to understand the specific needs, opportunities and challenges that exist for your organization. We take a close look at your content and data to help us make calculated technical and design choices throughout the process.

  • Design the solution

    Through a collaborative design process, we work closely with our clients to design the best solutions to their unique challenges.

    Using the information gathered through our research, we establish an information architecture based on strong organizational knowledge. With a deep understanding of your end users, we work to create a user experience model which best matches their needs and expectations. Using established best practices for user interface design and accessibility, we work to deliver a strong design system which is effective and delightful to use.

  • Build the right thing

    Using leading open source technologies and the Agile development methodology, Therefore thrives to solve problems the right way.

    Working with modern frameworks we build secure, scalable and stable systems for our clients. To empower your organization to structure, manage and disseminate their content across multiple channels, Therefore is specialized in building content management solutions with Drupal. Working with the Agile methodology allows Therefore to adapt quickly to the changing needs of your digital strategy and maintain flexibility through the entire project life cycle.
